# Sushi Bowl (Poke Bowl)

All the flavors of sushi — no rolling required. Quick, healthy, endlessly customizable.

## Ingredients (2 servings)

### Rice
- 200 g sushi rice
- 2 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1/2 tsp salt

### Toppings
- 200 g fresh salmon (sashimi-grade) or tuna, cubed
- 1 avocado (sliced)
- 1 small cucumber (thinly sliced)
- 100 g edamame (thawed)
- 2 tbsp pickled ginger (gari)
- 1 nori sheet (cut into strips)
- 1 tbsp toasted sesame seeds
- Spring onions (sliced)

### Fish Marinade
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tsp rice vinegar
- 1 tsp honey or mirin

### Spicy Mayo (optional)
- 2 tbsp Japanese mayo (Kewpie)
- 1 tsp Sriracha

## Instructions

1. **Cook rice:** Wash sushi rice until water runs clear. Combine with 250 ml water, bring to boil, reduce heat, cover and simmer 12 minutes. Rest 10 minutes. Mix vinegar, sugar and salt, fold into warm rice.

2. **Marinate fish:** Cube the salmon, toss with soy sauce, sesame oil, rice vinegar and honey. Let sit 10-15 minutes.

3. **Spicy mayo:** Mix Kewpie mayo with Sriracha.

4. **Assemble bowls:** Divide rice into bowls. Arrange salmon, avocado, cucumber, edamame and ginger on top. Sprinkle with nori strips, sesame seeds and spring onions. Drizzle with spicy mayo.

## Variations

- **Vegetarian:** Replace salmon with mango and extra avocado
- **Teriyaki chicken:** Grilled chicken with teriyaki glaze instead of raw fish
- **Spicy tuna:** Mix tuna with Sriracha and mayo

## Tips

- Buy sashimi-grade fish from a fishmonger (not regular supermarket fish counter)
- Wet your hands when portioning rice to prevent sticking
- Great for meal prep — keep components separate and assemble fresh

## Nutrition (per serving)
- Calories: ~580 kcal
- Prep time: 30 minutes
- Difficulty: Easy
